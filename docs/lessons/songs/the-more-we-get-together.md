# SONG: The More We Get Together

## Lyrics

**Verse 1:**
The more we get together, together, together
The more we get together, the happier we'll be
For your friends are my friends, and my friends are your friends
The more we get together, the happier we'll be

**Verse 2:**
The more we laugh together, together, together
The more we laugh together, the happier we'll be
For your joy is my joy, and my joy is your joy
The more we laugh together, the happier we'll be

**Verse 3:**
The more we play together, together, together
The more we play together, the happier we'll be
For your fun is my fun, and my fun is your fun
The more we play together, the happier we'll be

## ASL Signs

Learn these signs to use while singing:

- **TOGETHER** - Hold both hands with fingers spread open and pointing forward at chest level. Move them toward each other until the fingers are close or touching, showing unity and closeness.

- **HAPPY** - Place both hands on your chest with your fingers spread. Brush both hands upward and outward in a circular motion several times, as if spreading happiness from your heart.

- **FRIEND** - Hook your index and middle fingers together on both hands and rotate them toward yourself, interlocking the fingers as if representing the connection between two people.

- **LAUGH** - Starting with your index fingers pointing to the corners of your mouth, move both hands upward while spreading all fingers out, as if laughter is spreading from your mouth.

- **PLAY** - Make a Y-shape with both hands (thumbs and pinkies extended) and bend and wiggle both arms repeatedly, showing playful movement and fun.

- **LOVE** - Cross both hands over your heart, then open them outward slightly while keeping palms on your chest, showing affection coming from your heart.

- **MORE** - Touch the fingertips of both hands together (pinched hands), then move them toward each other and touch the fingertips again, showing the concept of "additional" or "more."

- **SMILE** - Using both index fingers, trace an upward line along your cheeks from the corners of your mouth upward, creating the shape of a smile on your face.

## Song Overview

"The More We Get Together" is a warm, inclusive friendship song that celebrates togetherness and social connection. Young children respond to its positive message about friendship and its simple, repetitive melody that allows everyone to participate regardless of singing ability. **Origin**: This traditional children's song originated as a folk melody and has been used since at least the early 1900s in various children's songbooks and kindergarten curricula. The song became widely popular through children's television programs and continues to be a staple at school gatherings, family events, and early childhood settings worldwide.

**Duration**: About 1-2 minutes

## Cultural Notes

This song is known and loved in English-speaking countries around the world, including the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia. Many cultures have adapted the melody and lyrics to include their own values and languages. In some European countries, versions emphasize community and cooperation in slightly different ways. The song's universal theme of friendship and happiness makes it easily translatable, and many non-English versions maintain the same cheerful message. Teachers and caregivers often use this song as a closing activity to reinforce group bonding and positive community values among young learners.
