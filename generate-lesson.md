## Guidelines for Quality

### **Developmental Appropriateness**
- All activities should be safe and suitable for 18 months to 4 years
- Instructions should be simple and flexible
- Expect short attention spans (5-15 minutes for focused activities)
- Emphasize exploration over perfection

### **Accessibility**
- Use common household materials when possible
- Provide alternatives for different abilities/resources
- Include sensory-friendly options
- Consider different learning styles

### **Educational Value**
- Connect abstract concepts to concrete experiences
- Encourage natural curiosity and questions
- Build on previous knowledge when possible
- Make learning joyful and playful

### **Practical Implementation**
- Be realistic about mess and cleanup
- Provide clear, actionable steps
- Anticipate common challenges
- Include ways to involve siblings or peers
